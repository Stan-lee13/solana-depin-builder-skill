# Regulatory Enforcement — Incident Response Runbook

## Severity: Critical

## Trigger Conditions
- Received regulatory notice (SEC, CFTC, etc.)
- Received cease-and-desist order
- Exchange delisting due to regulatory pressure
- Subpoena for documents or testimony
- Regulatory inquiry about token classification

## Immediate Actions (0-15 minutes)

1. **Declare Incident**
   - Alert legal team immediately
   - Alert executive team
   - Create incident channel: `#incident-regulatory`
   - Set incident severity: Critical

2. **Secure Communications**
   - All incident communication in privileged channels
   - No public statements without legal approval
   - Preserve all relevant documents and communications
   - Enable legal hold on all communications

3. **Assess Notice**
   - Read and understand the regulatory notice
   - Identify deadlines for response
   - Identify requested information
   - Identify potential consequences

## Short-Term Actions (15-60 minutes)

4. **Engage Legal Counsel**
   - Contact external legal counsel if needed
   - Share regulatory notice with legal team
   - Discuss strategy and response options
   - Determine if immediate action required

5. **Preserve Evidence**
   - Backup all relevant data
   - Preserve all communications
   - Document all actions taken
   - Secure all relevant contracts and agreements

6. **Assess Impact**
   - Evaluate impact on operations
   - Evaluate impact on token listing
   - Evaluate impact on operators
   - Evaluate financial impact

## Medium-Term Actions (1-24 hours)

7. **Prepare Response**
   - Work with legal team on response
   - Gather requested documents
   - Prepare factual responses to inquiries
   - Review response with legal counsel before submitting

8. **Internal Communication**
   - Inform key stakeholders (under legal privilege)
   - Prepare internal FAQ for team
   - Designate spokesperson for external communication
   - Prepare holding statement for media

9. **Operational Continuity**
   - Assess if operations can continue
   - If operations must pause: Plan pause procedure
   - If operations can continue: Document compliance measures
   - Review all operational procedures for compliance

## Long-Term Actions (1-7 days)

10. **Compliance Review**
    - Conduct full compliance audit
    - Review all tokenomics for securities law compliance
    - Review all marketing materials
    - Review all operator communications

11. **Adjust Strategy**
    - If required: Adjust tokenomics to comply
    - If required: Adjust marketing materials
    - If required: Adjust operational procedures
    - If required: Consider jurisdiction migration

12. **Community Communication**
    - When legally appropriate: Communicate with community
    - Be transparent about situation (within legal bounds)
    - Reassure community about network stability
    - Provide updates on resolution progress

## Recovery Indicators

- Regulatory matter resolved
- Compliance measures implemented
- Operations continue normally
- Community confidence restored
- No further regulatory action

## Escalation

If regulatory action threatens network survival:
- Consider jurisdiction migration
- Consider protocol restructuring
- Consider token migration
- Prepare for potential shutdown

## Prevention

- Regular legal review of tokenomics
- Regular legal review of marketing materials
- Maintain relationships with regulators
- Stay informed on regulatory developments
- Implement compliance measures proactively
- Maintain legal counsel in key jurisdictions
